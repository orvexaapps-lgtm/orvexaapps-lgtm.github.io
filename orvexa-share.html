Privacy and permissions

Orvexa Share is designed for local device-to-device transfer. The developer has indicated that this app does not collect or transfer user data to third parties.

Permissions support network discovery, Wi-Fi multicast, sockets, foreground transfer services, notifications, external storage management, and media file access on compatible Android and Fire OS devices.

Supported sharing scenarios

Fire TV setup

Move APKs, videos, ZIP archives, documents, and photos from an Android phone or tablet to a Fire TV device over your local connection.

Phone and tablet transfer

Send files between Android devices without uploading content to a remote cloud account.

Hotspot workflows

Use hotspot-based sharing when a standard Wi-Fi network is not available, depending on device support and permissions.
