Storage Cleanup

How to safely delete junk files on Android

The safest cleanup is careful, not aggressive. Delete files you can identify, clear cache selectively, and avoid wiping app data unless you understand what will be lost.

Safe to review first

Old APKs

Delete installers after the app works, unless you intentionally keep a versioned backup.

Failed transfers

Remove partial files created by interrupted Wi-Fi or USB transfers.

Duplicate media

Compare size and date before deleting similar videos or photos.

Cache

Clear cache for large apps when they misbehave or consume too much storage.

Do not delete blindly

  • App data folders if you rely on saved settings or downloads.
  • Documents you have not backed up.
  • Folders with names you do not recognize until you identify the related app.
  • Recently transferred files before confirming they opened correctly.
