About Orvexa Files Pro

Orvexa Files Pro is a premium file manager built for users who want a clean, fast, and reliable storage management experience across Android phones, tablets, and Fire TV devices.

The application includes powerful built-in tools allowing users to manage documents, images, videos, ZIP archives, PDFs, APK files, and media playback without requiring multiple external apps.

Optimized remote navigation support makes the app highly usable on Fire TV and Android TV devices while maintaining excellent touch support on mobile devices.
