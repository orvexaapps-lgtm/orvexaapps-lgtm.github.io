How to use IPTV and M3U playlists with Orvexa TV

Orvexa TV is a modern IPTV and M3U player that lets you add custom playlists, import local files, and manage channels. This guide covers setup, playlist management, and troubleshooting for Android TV, Fire TV, phones, and tablets.

What are IPTV and M3U playlists?

IPTV stands for Internet Protocol Television, which delivers TV content over internet networks instead of traditional broadcast methods. An M3U is a playlist format (plain text file) that contains channel information and stream URLs. Orvexa TV plays M3U playlists containing IPTV channels and other streaming content.

Important: Orvexa TV does not provide channels. You are responsible for adding legal, authorized content only.

How to add a playlist URL to Orvexa TV

  1. Open Orvexa TV on your device.
  2. Go to Settings or Playlist menu (layout varies by device).
  3. Select "Add Playlist" or "Add URL".
  4. Paste the M3U playlist URL into the input field.
  5. Confirm and save. The app will load the playlist and show available channels.
  6. Tap a channel to start playback with advanced player controls.

How to import a local M3U file

  1. Save an M3U file to your device storage (phone, tablet, or Android TV).
  2. Open Orvexa TV and select "Import Playlist" or "Import Local File".
  3. Browse to the M3U file location using the file picker.
  4. Select the M3U file. Orvexa TV will parse it and add all channels.
  5. Start playing any channel immediately or save favorites for quick access.

Managing playlists and favorites

  • Multiple playlists: Add several URL or local playlists and switch between them.
  • Favorites: Mark frequently watched channels as favorites for faster access.
  • Recently watched: Resume playback from where you left off.
  • EPG support: When available, view program guides and schedule information.
  • Search: Find specific channels quickly in large playlists.

Device-specific notes

Android TV and Fire TV: Use remote control to navigate. The app is optimized for TV screens with large touch targets and responsive remote button handling.

Fire Tablets: Tablet interface provides touch-friendly channel browsing and playlist management with full feature support.

Android phones: Use the phone interface with touch controls optimized for smaller screens. All features remain accessible.

Advanced features

  • Video quality selection for adaptive streaming
  • Multi-audio track support for multilingual content
  • Advanced player controls: pause, resume, seek, and fast-forward
  • Material Design 3 UI with modern interface

FAQ

Where do I find M3U playlist URLs?

M3U playlists are often shared by IPTV service providers, content organizations, or communities. Verify sources before adding, and ensure any content you use is legal and authorized.

Why is my playlist not loading?

Check that the URL is correct, your internet connection is active, and the playlist server is online. Try the same URL in a web browser first. If it works there but not in Orvexa TV, contact support.

Can I use multiple playlists at once?

Yes. Add multiple URL or local playlists and switch between them. The app stores your preferences and playback history.

Does Orvexa TV keep my playlists after restart?

Yes. Playlists are saved locally on your device and persist after app restarts or device reboots.

How do I remove a playlist?

Go to Playlist management in settings, select the playlist, and choose "Remove" or "Delete".
