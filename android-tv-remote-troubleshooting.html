Android TV remote troubleshooting

Remote problems can look like app problems. Before reinstalling an app, check pairing, batteries, Bluetooth interference, accessibility services, and whether the app was designed for TV focus navigation.

Quick fixes

  1. Replace or recharge batteries and restart the TV.
  2. Move the remote closer and remove objects blocking the TV or streaming stick.
  3. Unpair and pair the remote again from system settings.
  4. Test navigation in the Android TV home screen and another app.
  5. If only one app has focus issues, contact the app developer with the device model and Android version.

FAQ

Why does the remote work on the home screen but not in an app?

The app may not fully support TV focus navigation. Orvexa TV-oriented pages call out remote-friendly behavior where it is part of the app design.

Can a phone remote app help?

Yes. Phone remote apps are useful for typing passwords or links, but they may not fix Bluetooth pairing problems with the physical remote.
