Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026. This policy explains how Orvexa Apps handles privacy across this website and our Android, Fire TV, and Fire Tablet applications.

View Terms Contact Support

1. Information we collect

Orvexa Apps does not directly request or store personal account information through this website. Some applications may use third-party services for advertising, analytics, or crash diagnostics.

  • Advertising identifiers used for ad delivery or aggregated reporting.
  • Device type, operating system version, and app stability diagnostics.
  • Anonymous usage patterns that help us improve performance and UI behavior.

2. Third-party services

Applications may integrate providers such as Google AdMob or Start.io. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

  • Google AdMob Privacy
  • Start.io Privacy

3. How data is used

  • To improve stability, playback quality, and device compatibility.
  • To understand which features are useful and which flows need improvement.
  • To support advertising delivery where an app includes ad-supported functionality.
  • To reduce abuse, crashes, and obvious misuse patterns.

We do not sell personal data or share personal information for unrelated marketing purposes.

4. Local files and device storage

Apps such as file managers, media players, and Orvexa Share are primarily local-first. Your media and documents remain on your device unless you explicitly choose an external service or link controlled by a third party.

Orvexa Share is designed for local device-to-device file transfer over Wi-Fi or hotspot connections. The developer has indicated that Orvexa Share does not collect or transfer user data to third parties.

5. Children’s privacy

Our apps are not intentionally designed to collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been provided inappropriately, we will act to remove it where possible.

6. Your choices

  • Disable personalized advertising using your device settings where supported.
  • Control app permissions through Android or Fire OS system settings.
  • Contact us to ask questions about privacy expectations or policy updates.

7. Policy updates

We may revise this policy when products, integrations, or legal expectations change. Updates are posted on this page with a refreshed revision date.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to orvexaapps@gmail.com.
