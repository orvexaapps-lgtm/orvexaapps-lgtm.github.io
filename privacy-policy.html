Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Orvexa Apps ("we", "our", or "us") handles information and respects your privacy when you use our applications and website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly from users. However, our applications may use third-party services such as:

  • Ad networks (e.g., AdMob, StartIO) - for anonymous analytics and ad serving
  • Analytics services - for understanding app usage patterns
  • Crash reporting tools - for app stability improvements

These services may collect limited, anonymized device information such as:

  • Advertising ID (for ad targeting only)
  • Device type and OS version
  • App crash and error logs
  • Usage statistics and feature interaction

2. Third-Party Services

Our applications may integrate third-party services and advertising networks. These services operate under their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob Privacy
  • StartApp Privacy

3. How We Use Information

Any collected data is used solely for:

  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Understanding app usage patterns
  • Serving relevant advertisements
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

All files managed by Orvexa applications remain on your device. We do not:

  • Upload or store your personal files on external servers
  • Sync your data to cloud services without explicit permission
  • Back up your application data without consent

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that a child has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of analytics collection (where available)
  • Disable personalized advertising in your device settings

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or applicable regulations. We will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the revised policy on this page. Your continued use of our applications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
