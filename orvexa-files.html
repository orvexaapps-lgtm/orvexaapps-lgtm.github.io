About Orvexa Files

Orvexa Files is a fast and modern file manager created to provide smooth file browsing and storage management across Android devices and Fire TV systems. The app is optimized for performance, simple navigation, TV remote support, and lightweight resource usage.

Whether you want to manage videos, music, documents, APK files, or folders, Orvexa Files gives you a clean and responsive experience without unnecessary complexity.
