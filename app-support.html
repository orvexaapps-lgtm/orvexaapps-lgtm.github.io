Before contacting support

  1. Note the device model, Android version, and app version.
  2. Describe exactly what you tapped before the problem appeared.
  3. Check storage and app permissions in Android settings.
  4. Try restarting the app and device once.
  5. Use Contact for direct support when the issue remains.
