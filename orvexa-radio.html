About Orvexa Radio

Orvexa Radio is a lightweight internet radio streaming application built for users who enjoy discovering global music, podcasts, live radio, and news content.

The app offers fast station discovery with optimized playback performance and smooth navigation for both touch devices and TV remote controls.

Users can search thousands of public stations from the Radio Browser community and build their own personalized favorites collection.

The interface focuses on simplicity, fast navigation, and uninterrupted listening across Android and Fire OS devices.
