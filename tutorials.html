Tutorial FAQ

Which guide should beginners read first?

Start with the beginner Android TV setup guide, then read the storage basics and APK safety guides before sideloading apps.

Do these guides work for Fire TV?

Many concepts apply to Fire TV because it is Android-based, but menus and store policies may differ by device and Fire OS version.

Are the guides only for Orvexa apps?

No. The tutorials are written as general Android education, with Orvexa apps linked where they are relevant to the task.
