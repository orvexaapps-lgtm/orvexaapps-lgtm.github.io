Fix buffering and storage issues on Android TV

Buffering is not always an internet problem. Android TV can also stall when internal storage is nearly full, app cache is damaged, USB storage is slow, or the media format is difficult for the device to decode.

Diagnose the cause

SymptomLikely causeTry first
Online video buffersWi-Fi or app cacheRestart router, clear streaming app cache, test another app.
Local video stuttersSlow USB drive or unsupported codecCopy to internal storage or try another file format.
Apps freezeLow internal storageDelete duplicate downloads and unused apps.

Fix sequence

  1. Check free storage before changing network settings.
  2. Restart the TV and router to clear temporary network and playback issues.
  3. Clear cache for the problem app, but avoid clearing app data unless necessary.
  4. Move large local media to USB or another device.
  5. Use Orvexa Media Player for simple local playback tests.
