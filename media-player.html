Product Description

Orvexa Media Player is a fast and modern media player built for users who want a simple and reliable local playback experience.

Enjoy smooth playback for locally stored audio and video files with an optimized interface designed for both touch controls and TV remotes.

The application focuses on speed, lightweight performance, privacy, and a clean viewing experience across Android phones, tablets, and Fire TV devices.
