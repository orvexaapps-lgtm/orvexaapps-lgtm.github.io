Android app permission explanations

Permissions are Android's way of asking whether an app may access a feature or data area. A permission is not automatically bad, but it should match the app's purpose.

Common permissions

PermissionWhy an app may need it
Storage or mediaFile managers and media players need it to show local files.
NetworkRadio apps, ads, updates, downloads, and transfer features may need it.
NotificationsLong transfers or background playback may show progress or controls.
Install unknown appsFile managers may request it only when you choose to install an APK.

Good permission habits

  • Review permission prompts before tapping allow.
  • Use Android settings to remove permissions you no longer want.
  • Read the app page and privacy policy when a permission is unclear.
